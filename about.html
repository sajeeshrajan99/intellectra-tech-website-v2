Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

All FAQ
What digital marketing services do you offer?

We provide a complete suite of Digital Marketing Services, including SEO, Google Ads (PPC), Social Media Marketing (SMM), Content Marketing, Branding, Web & App Development, and Custom Solutions — all designed to drive measurable growth.

Where are your services available?

We’re based in the UK and work with clients nationwide. We also serve international businesses looking to expand their reach in UK and global markets through digital channels.

How much should I budget to start working with Intellectra?

Our pricing is flexible and depends entirely on your goals and the services you need. After a free consultation, we’ll create a custom solution plan that fits your budget — whether you’re a small startup or a large company.

Do you guarantee specific search rankings or immediate results?

No, ethical agencies cannot guarantee exact SEO rankings or immediate traffic. However, we guarantee proven, data-driven strategies and transparent reporting that aim for the maximum possible growth for your investment.

How long will it take to see results?

Results depend on your chosen service and goals. Paid Ads can deliver leads within days, while SEO and organic strategies usually take 3–6 months. We ensure both short-term wins and long-term growth.

Can you help if I already have a website or marketing team?

Yes. We collaborate with your existing team or improve your current setup. Whether you need a website revamp, SEO optimization, or campaign management, we can enhance what you already have — without disrupting ongoing work.

How will I track my campaign’s progress?

You’ll have full visibility through performance dashboards, weekly updates, and monthly reports. We track key metrics like traffic, conversions, ROI, and engagement — all shared in real time for complete transparency.

What if I need to upgrade or modify my plan?

No problem. Our packages are flexible, and you can scale or modify your plan anytime with prior notice. We adapt to your business pace — not the other way around.
